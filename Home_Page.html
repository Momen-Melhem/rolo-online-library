About Us



Welcome to our educational library! We are students from Applied Science University, and this project is our way of combining creativity,
knowledge, and teamwork to make a difference. Our library is a small online platform designed to share educationaltopics and help others access valuable
knowledge easily. It’s not just a project—it’s our contribution to spreading ideas and inspiring others to learn.

We are a team of three passionate students, each bringing a unique perspective and creative touch:

Zaid: Focused on Islamic literature and political books, exploring the balance between faith, governance, and societal values.

Mousa: Dedicated to human development and financial topics, blending personal growth strategies with practical economic insights.

Momen: Specializes in technology, guiding users on building PCs and installing Windows systems, making complex tasks easy to grasp.

Working together, we designed and coded this website entirely using HTML and CSS, learning and growing as a team along the way. Each of us contributed to this platform
with care and determination, hoping to make it a valuable resource for anyone who visits.

Thank you for stopping by and supporting our journey. Explore, learn, and let this library be a stepping stone toward your own growth and curiosity! We’re excited to
share our work with you, and we hope it leaves a lasting impression.
